How It Works
Our deep learning pipeline extracts unique ridge patterns from fingerprints and correlates them with blood group markers using a trained CNN model.
01
Input Capture
Upload a high-resolution fingerprint image or place your finger on the optical scanner for live biometric capture.
02
Ridge Extraction
Advanced image processing isolates minutiae points — ridge endings and bifurcations — from the fingerprint's unique pattern.
03
CNN Analysis
Our convolutional neural network processes 128+ feature vectors against 5,000+ trained fingerprint samples per blood type.
04
Result Output
Blood group (ABO + Rh) is determined with 93% accuracy, along with compatibility information and confidence score.
